[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's take a look at the system architecture. When a lead email comes in, it first enters the security checkpoint node. Here, it is scrubbed for PII like credit cards and SSNs, and audited for competitor domains or prompt injections. (pause)
If it is safe, it passes to the lead coordinator. This is our orchestrator LLM agent. It runs a ReAct loop to call specialized sub-agents: the email parser, which extracts contact and company details, and the lead scorer, which queries company profiles and scores the lead out of 100. (pause)
The lead coordinator also connects directly to our Model Context Protocol CRM server to query and save records. (pause)
Next, the routing node directs the lead. Borderline leads scored between 40 and 69 are sent to a human review node, which pauses the workflow for administrator approval. High-quality leads are automatically approved and routed to the final output node to generate the report. (pause)
